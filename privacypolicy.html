Privacy Policy for Orbit Chrome Extension

Effective Date: October 7, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Privacy Policy for the Orbit Chrome Extension ("Extension"). Orbit is a Chrome extension designed to help users track their YouTube subscriptions and manage their viewing preferences. This policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use the Extension.

By using the Orbit Extension, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the Extension.

2. Data Collection

The Orbit Extension collects the following types of data to provide its services:

  • Account Information: When you log in to the Extension, we collect your email address and password. This information is used solely for authentication purposes to access your YouTube account data.
  • YouTube Subscription Data: The Extension accesses and tracks your YouTube subscriptions, including channel names, subscription dates, and related metadata. This data is retrieved directly from your YouTube account via the YouTube API.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics, such as how often the Extension is used, to improve functionality and performance.

We do not collect any personally identifiable information beyond what is necessary for the Extension's core functionality. All data collection is performed in compliance with Google's Chrome Extension policies and the YouTube API terms of service.

3. Data Usage and Purpose

The data collected by the Orbit Extension is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To authenticate your account and enable access to YouTube subscription tracking features.
  • To display and manage your YouTube subscriptions within the Extension's interface.
  • To provide personalized recommendations or notifications based on your subscription activity.
  • To improve the Extension's performance, troubleshoot issues, and develop new features.

We do not use your data for marketing, advertising, or any other purposes outside of providing and enhancing the Extension's services.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and access controls:

  • Local Storage: Account credentials and subscription data are stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage APIs. We do not transmit or store your password on external servers.
  • Server Storage: Anonymized usage data may be stored on our servers for analytics purposes, but this data cannot be linked back to individual users.
  • Security Measures: We implement HTTPS encryption for all data transmissions, regular security audits, and access restrictions to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

While we strive to protect your data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to using best practices to safeguard your information.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • YouTube API: Data is shared with Google/YouTube solely for the purpose of retrieving your subscription information, as required by the YouTube API.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or legal process.
  • Service Providers: We may use trusted third-party services for hosting or analytics, but only under strict confidentiality agreements and with data anonymization where possible.

All third-party interactions are governed by their respective privacy policies and terms of service.

6. User Rights and Data Deletion

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: You can view the data collected by the Extension through its interface or by contacting us.
  • Correction: You can update your account information directly within the Extension.
  • Deletion: You can request the deletion of your data by uninstalling the Extension or contacting us. Upon request, we will delete all stored data associated with your account within 30 days.
  • Opt-Out: You can stop data collection by disabling or uninstalling the Extension at any time.

To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related inquiries, please contact us using the information provided below.

  • Other applicable privacy laws and regulations
